How it works

From thought to insight in three steps

Legate turns your raw thoughts into a living knowledge base you can explore, search, and build on over time.

01
Capture

Record a voice memo (Motif) or upload text from anywhere. Legate's AI transcribes, categorizes, and extracts structured knowledge automatically. No organizing required.

02
Connect

Your knowledge automatically forms a graph. See how your ideas relate to each other in a force-directed, interactive knowledge map. New entries find their place in the graph instantly.

03
Explore

Browse, search, or chat with your growing library. Ask your AI questions about your own knowledge, or use MCP to query it from Claude Desktop and other AI tools — your second brain, always within reach.
