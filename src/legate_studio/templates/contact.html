Tips

Getting a useful response faster

For bug reports, include:

  • What you were trying to do
  • What happened instead
  • Your browser and OS (e.g., "Chrome on macOS")
  • Any error messages you saw

For feature requests, tell us what workflow you're trying to accomplish — not just the feature itself. Understanding the underlying need helps us build the right thing.

For billing questions, include your account username so we can look up your subscription quickly.
