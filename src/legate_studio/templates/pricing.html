What is BYOK?

Bring Your Own Key — what it means and why it's different

BYOK stands for Bring Your Own Key. Instead of Legate bundling AI usage into a higher subscription price, you connect your own Google Gemini API key and Legate uses it directly.

When a Motif is processed — transcribed, categorized, and structured — that's an AI call. When you run semantic search, that's another. With BYOK, those calls go to Google using your API key, and Google bills you directly at their standard rates.

Google's Gemini Flash API is inexpensive — typical personal use costs a few cents per month. You pay Google for the actual compute, and you pay Legate $0.99/month for the platform. No markup, no bundling, no guessing about hidden AI costs.

The Managed tier is the simpler option: we provide the AI, nothing to configure, and it all works immediately. Managed costs more because we're absorbing the AI costs on your behalf.

How to get a Gemini API key

Visit Google AI Studio, sign in with a Google account, and create an API key. It takes about two minutes. Then paste it into your Legate account settings — that's it.
